INTERNSHIP WITH LIVE PROJECTS GRPL INDIA

Internship – Live project Training Lucknow

Galaxy Resource Private Limited will provide you an opportunity to work with the team of Software Developers, under the guidance of project managers. Our core focus is to make the students well verse with the different cycles of Software Development and to provide them hand on experience on LIVE PROJECTS. We shall provide experience letter for the said duration.

Passed Out Students ⁄ Students of B.E., B.Tech, B.Sc, BCA | MCA, M.SC | IT Graduates | IT Aspirants

Our core focus is:

  • To groom the candidate technically & interpersonally by working on live projects (PHP or Software Testing).
  • To effectively mentor the candidate for taking challenges and resolving them successfully within a project.

The prerequisite and eligibility for joining the project internship are:

  • Candidate must be student of MCA/M.Tech/BE/B.TECH/BCA/Bsc(IT/CS)/Msc(IT/CS)/MBA(IT)/MBA(System).
  • Candidate must have good analytical and logical skills.
  • Candidate must have basic computer skills.
  • Candidate must have command over one computer programming language/concepts(C/C++).
  • Candidate must have Knowledge of HTML and SQL

Duration:

  • Six months, Self support basis.

Interested candidates can walk-in to our office from Mon-Fri between 10 am to 6 pm along with their latest Resume, Training/Internship Letter from the college (if applicable), Identity Proof and one passport size photograph.
OR
Interested candidates mail to liveproject@grplindia.com with subject ” Intership with Live project & should mention the Course Name ” and updated CV.
